1 hour = 3 logbook hours

Every one hour of your daytime driving with a licensed driving instructor counts for 3 hours in your logbook. A maximum of 10 hours will be recorded as 30 hours in your logbook.

Driving lessons at night (between sunset and sunrise) will only count 1 hour of night driving. The other 2 hours will be added to daytime driving hours.

Learn in your car

You may learn in your own car if it is registered, roadworthy and are comprehensively insured.

You should be a confident learner driver and ready for the driving test.

We suggest you have one or more driving lessons in the instructor car prior.

International drivers license

To easily convert your overseas drivers license, simply book either a driving lesson or driving test package.

There are no extra charges applied. Lessons will be charged at the normal rate.
